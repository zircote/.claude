# HMH Brand Guidelines

Brand guidelines for HMH (formerly Houghton Mifflin Harcourt), the adaptive learning company serving K-12 education.

## Company Overview

**Official Name:** HMH (rebranded July 18, 2024)
**Industry:** K-12 Adaptive Learning / EdTech
**Headquarters:** Boston Financial District
**Founded:** 1832
**CEO:** Jack Lynch

**Market Position:** 90% of U.S. K-12 schools; 50M students in 150 countries; 4M+ educators served.

## Brand Essence

### Big Idea: "Growth in Motion"

### Position

HMH is an **adaptive learning company** (not a textbook publisher). Digital-first innovator with dynamic solutions for modern education.

### Core Capabilities (Logo Symbolism)

1. **Curriculum** - Research-backed instructional programs
2. **Assessment** - Data-driven insights (includes NWEA)
3. **Professional Development** - Educator support

## Visual Identity

### Logo

2024 logo: **Three interlocking ovals** in upward trajectory representing curriculum/assessment/professional development and students/teachers/administrators. Shapes symbolize constant motion, change, and growth.

**Usage:** Maintain clear space; do not distort, recolor, or add effects; minimum 0.5" print / 50px digital.

### Colors

**Primary:** Hot pink to fiery orange gradient (hyper-gradient spectrum)
- Symbolizes dynamism, energy, innovation, continuous growth
- Use for hero content and key brand moments

**Style:** Clean, modern appearance; avoid excessive gradients/shadows; flat colors for versatility.

### Typography

**Primary Typeface:** Micro
- Rounded tops, flat bottoms
- Balances warmth and seriousness
- Lowercase adds friendliness

**Guidelines:** Clean, simple, readable across platforms; meet WCAG AA accessibility.

### Visual Style

**Photography:** Warm, human feel; celebrates student uniqueness and teacher heroism; authentic classroom moments.

**Illustration:** Contemporary, engaging, dynamic; complements photography.

## Brand Voice: "Creative Co-Pilot"

Experienced, dynamic force with passion for what works. Skilled, steadfast partner providing vital tools, information, and decisive guidance exactly when needed.

### Voice Attributes

- **Experienced:** Nearly 200 years of educational expertise
- **Dynamic:** Innovative, forward-looking
- **Passionate:** Genuine care for success
- **Practical:** Evidence-based, focused on what works
- **Empowering:** Supports educators, doesn't replace them

### Tone

**Do:** Lead with benefits; use active language; show empathy for educator challenges; connect technology to human outcomes.

**Don't:** Sound like a textbook company; use unexplained jargon; focus solely on technology over human connection.

## Messaging Framework

### Primary Messages

1. **Growth for Every Student:** "Create growth for every student, no matter the circumstances."
2. **Adaptive Learning:** "Learning that adapts to student needs and identifies ways to progress."
3. **Educator Partnership:** "Equipping educators with resources to focus on individual needs and relationships."

### Proof Points

- 90% of U.S. K-12 schools
- 50M students in 150 countries
- Integrated platform (curriculum + assessment + professional learning)
- NWEA assessment; Writable AI writing assistant
- Research-backed programs

## Content Standards

### Product Names

**Core:** Into Reading, Into Literature, Into Math, Into Science, Into Social Studies
**Assessment:** NWEA MAP
**Intervention:** Read 180, System 44, Math 180, English 3D
**Platform:** HMH Performance Suite

### Style

- Sentence case headlines
- Oxford comma: yes
- Numbers: spell one-nine; numerals 10+
- Grade levels: K-12 (hyphen)
- Always "HMH" (never H.M.H.)

### Accessibility

All content: WCAG 2.1 AA; alt text for images; sufficient color contrast; proper heading hierarchy; captioned videos.
